Transportation activities and electricity generation are the largest sources of CO
emissions. Agricultural activities, which include GHG emissions from livestock
operations and soil management, are the largest sources of CH4 and N,O emissions in
Arizona, respectively. Hydrofluorocarbon (HFC) emissions occur primarily from the
substitution (replacement) of ozone depleting substances in air conditioning and
refrigeration equipment. HFCs are also a significant source of statewide GHG
