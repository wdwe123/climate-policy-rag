CITY OF GLENDALE'S
STORMWATER MANAGEMENT PROGRAM
The City of Glendale (referred to as City throughout this document) is a leader in protecting the
environment and implementing sustainability programs, such as the City's stormwater
management program. Through the City's stormwater management program, the City strives
to prevent pollution to the municipally-owned separate storm sewer system. It is "separate" in
that the stormwater conveyance system is not connected to the sanitary sewer system and
therefore, not connected to a treatment plant. As shown in Figure 1, stormwater that does not
soak into the ground or evaporate discharges to washes or rivers without treatment.
